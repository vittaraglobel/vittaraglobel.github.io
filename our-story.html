The Founder's Story

Darshik Pratapbhai Fataniya

Growing up in Porbandar, Gujarat — the birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi — Darshik developed a deep respect for integrity, hard work, and service. These values are the foundation of everything Vittara stands for.

After completing his B.Com, Darshik pursued advanced qualifications in finance — earning an MBA (Finance) and an MSc in Accounting & Finance, with a specific focus on UK financial markets, HMRC regulations, and FCA compliance.

In 2024, Darshik founded Vittara with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between UK businesses that need premium financial services and India's exceptional pool of qualified accounting talent — at a price point that genuinely levels the playing field for growing businesses.

"I started Vittara because I saw too many great UK businesses being held back by expensive accountants or poor financial advice. I knew we could do better — and deliver more value."

Our Journey

How Vittara Was Built

2019–2021

The Academic Foundation

Darshik completes his B.Com, then pursues an MBA in Finance — developing deep expertise in financial management, corporate accounting, and business strategy.

2021–2023

MSc Accounting & Finance — UK Focus

Specialising in UK-specific accounting standards, HMRC regulations, FCA compliance, and financial reporting — building the technical foundation for serving UK clients.

Early 2024

Vittara is Founded

Vittara is established in Porbandar, Gujarat — with a clear mission to deliver UK-quality financial services at India-competitive pricing, starting with accounting, bookkeeping, and paraplanning.

Mid 2024

First UK Clients Onboarded

The first UK clients come on board — an e-commerce business, an IFA practice, and a property investor. All three see significant results within the first quarter.

2024–2025

Expanding to Australia & UAE

Building on UK success, Vittara extends its services to Australian and UAE businesses — adding BAS lodgements, GST returns, FTA compliance, and corporate tax to the service offering.

Today

Growing the Team & Impact

Vittara continues to grow — hiring dedicated, hand-picked team members in Gujarat and expanding the service range to serve more UK, Australian, and UAE businesses.
