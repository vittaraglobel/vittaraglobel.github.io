Complaints Policy

We take every complaint seriously. Our goal is to resolve your concern quickly, fairly, and transparently — every time.

Our Process

How We Handle Complaints

We follow a clear, structured process to ensure your complaint is handled with the care it deserves.

1
Day 1

Submit Your Complaint

Use the form below, email us at [email protected], or write to our address. Please include as much detail as possible — what happened, when, and what outcome you're seeking.

2
Within 5 Business Days

Acknowledgement

We will send a written acknowledgement confirming we've received your complaint, along with a reference number and the name of the person handling your case.

3
Within 28 Days

Investigation & Resolution

We'll thoroughly investigate your complaint, reviewing all relevant records and correspondence. We aim to provide a full written response within 28 calendar days. If we need more time, we'll let you know.

4
Final

Final Response & Escalation Rights

If you're not satisfied with our response, you have the right to escalate to an independent body. Our final response letter will include details of your escalation options.
