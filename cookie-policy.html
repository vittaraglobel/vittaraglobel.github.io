Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

In plain English: We use cookies to make our website work properly, understand how visitors use it, and occasionally show relevant content. You're in control — you can manage your preferences below.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how people use the site.

Some cookies are essential for the website to function; others are optional and require your consent under UK law (PECR and UK GDPR).

Blocking Cookies via Your Browser

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Here's how:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of our website and client portal.
