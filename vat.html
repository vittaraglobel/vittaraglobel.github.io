VAT Schemes We Handle

We'll Find the Right Scheme for You

  • Standard

    Standard VAT Accounting

    The default scheme — VAT accounted for on invoices issued and received each quarter.

  • Cash-Based

    Cash Accounting Scheme

    VAT only accounted for when money changes hands — better cash flow for smaller businesses.

  • Flat Rate

    Flat Rate Scheme

    Pay a fixed percentage of turnover — often saving money for businesses with few VAT-bearing costs.

  • Annual

    Annual Accounting Scheme

    One VAT return per year with interim payments — reduces admin for eligible businesses.

  • OSS/IOSS

    VAT OSS & IOSS (E-Commerce)

    For online sellers shipping to EU customers — we manage the One Stop Shop returns.

VAT FAQs

You must register for VAT when your taxable turnover exceeds £90,000 in any rolling 12-month period. You can also register voluntarily below this threshold, which can be beneficial if your clients are VAT-registered businesses.
MTD for VAT requires all VAT-registered businesses to keep digital records and file returns using compatible software. We handle all MTD submissions through Xero or QuickBooks — fully compliant with HMRC requirements.
HMRC charges a default surcharge and potentially interest for late VAT filings. We can help bring your VAT record up to date and, in some cases, negotiate with HMRC to reduce or waive penalties for genuine first-time mistakes.
Yes. We handle VAT OSS (One Stop Shop) for EU cross-border sales, IOSS for low-value import consignments, and reverse charge accounting for B2B services. International VAT is complex — we specialise in it.
